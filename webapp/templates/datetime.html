naturaltime() - Relative Time

Return a natural representation of time relative to now.

Examples: "3 seconds ago" | "2 hours ago" | "5 days from now"

naturaldelta() - Time Duration

Return a natural representation of a time span (without "ago" or "from now").

Examples: "3 seconds" | "2 hours" | "5 days"

naturalday() - Natural Day

For dates that are today, tomorrow, or yesterday, return the natural text. Otherwise, return a formatted date.

Examples: "today" | "yesterday" | "tomorrow" | "Jun 05"

naturaldate() - Natural Date

Like naturalday, but appends year for dates outside current year.

Examples: "today" | "Jun 05" | "Jun 05 2020"

precisedelta() - Precise Time Delta

Return a precise representation of a time delta.

Examples: "2 days, 1 hour and 33.00 seconds" | "5 hours, 30 minutes and 15.50 seconds"

Tips:

  • naturaltime() is perfect for social media timestamps and activity feeds
  • naturaldelta() shows duration without temporal context
  • naturalday() is great for calendar applications
  • precisedelta() gives exact time breakdowns for detailed reports
